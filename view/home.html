Antidote Web Application

The Antidote Application (AA) is a computational system that automatically recommends individualized antidote treatment regimen and patient-specific doses based on patient-specific parameters. The AA is available in two formats: a standalone downloadable application for offline use and an online web application. It also provides a reporting solution linked to Poison Control and the Federal Adverse Event Reporting System. The system is well referenced and exclusively for toxicology. The antidotes and toxins covered by AA include those recommended for common use in hospitals as described by Dart et al, 2009.

To begin using the Antidote Application, click on the Antidotes or Toxins tabs in the upper right and follow the instructions.

This project was designed by Jon Long, R.Ph., and Allen Yingyuan Zhang under the direction of Guanglan Zhang, PhD., and Vladimir Brusic, PhD. as a part of the Boston University MET Health Informatics Lab program.
